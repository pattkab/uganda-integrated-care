Welcome to the Resource Hub for Integrated Health Services

In line with the Ministry of Health’s vision for Sustainability and Universal Health Coverage (UHC), this hub equips Ugandan health workers to advance integrated care—linking HIV, TB, NCD, and other services for efficient, sustainable, and patient-centred outcomes. Access key guidelines, training materials, and frameworks to implement integrated models at facility and community levels.

Built for frontline heroes: Download, read, and transform care delivery today.

About This Resource

This site about Integration serves as a centralized, accessible resource for Ugandan health workers to support the MoH's integration roadmap. It emphasizes sustainability in HIV care by linking vertical programs to holistic PHC. Future updates will include interactive tools, forums, and more.
