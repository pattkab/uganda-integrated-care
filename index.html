About This Hub

This GitHub Pages site serves as a centralized, accessible resource for Ugandan health workers to support the MoH's integration priorities. It emphasizes sustainability in HIV care by linking vertical programs to holistic PHC. Future updates may include interactive tools, forums, and more.
